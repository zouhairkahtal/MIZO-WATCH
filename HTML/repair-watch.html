The importance of precision and skill in time making

Watch repair includes diagnosing faults, disassembling and cleaning parts, and replacing damaged ones. It is reassembled and lubricated, then the watch is calibrated to set the precise time. The watch is tested to ensure proper performance, and finally cleaned and packed before being delivered to the customer.
